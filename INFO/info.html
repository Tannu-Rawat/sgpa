3. How to Calculate SGPA

The SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is calculated as follows:

  1. Multiply the grade points achieved in a course by the credit hours of that course.
  2. Add these results for all courses in the semester.
  3. Divide the total by the sum of the credit hours.

Formula:

SGPA = Σ(Ci × Gi) / ΣCi, where:

  • Ci = Number of credits for a course
  • Gi = Grade points earned for that course

4. How to Calculate CGPA

The CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the weighted mean of all the SGPAs achieved over multiple semesters. It is calculated using the formula:

Formula:

CGPA = Σ(SGPA × Semester Credits) / Σ(Total Semester Credits)

5. Important Points to Remember

  • Grades are important for tracking academic performance.
  • CGPA represents your overall performance throughout your academic career.
